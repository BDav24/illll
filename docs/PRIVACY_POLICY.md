# Privacy Policy — ILLLL

**Last updated:** March 2026

## The Short Version

ILLLL does not collect, transmit, or share any of your data. All data stays on your device.

## Health & Wellness Data

ILLLL accesses and stores the following health-related data that you manually enter within the app:

- **Habit completion status** — whether you completed daily health habits (e.g., exercise, breathing exercises, cold exposure, sleep, nutrition, social connection)
- **Streak data** — consecutive days of habit completion, calculated from your entries
- **Custom habit configurations** — habits you have added or modified
- **Breathing exercise data** — number of rounds and session details
- **Daily task entries** — personal tasks you create within the app

**How this data is used:** This data is used solely to display your progress, calculate streaks, and provide the habit tracking functionality of the app. It is never used for advertising, profiling, or any purpose other than the core app experience.

**How this data is stored:** All health data is stored locally on your device. This data is never transmitted to any server, cloud service, or third party. ILLLL does not have access to your data — only you do, on your device.

**How this data is shared:** It is not. ILLLL does not share, sell, or transfer any data to any third party, for any reason.

## What Data We Collect

ILLLL does not collect any data. The app does not transmit any information from your device. There are no servers, no accounts, and no data collection of any kind.

## Analytics & Tracking

None. ILLLL contains zero analytics, zero tracking pixels, zero crash reporting services, and zero third-party SDKs that collect data.

## Network Usage

ILLLL does not make any network requests. The app works entirely offline.

## Account

There is no account. There is no login. There is nothing to sign up for.

## Third-Party Services

ILLLL does not integrate with any third-party services that collect user data.

## Data Deletion

All your data is stored locally on your device. To delete it, use "Reset All Data" in Settings, or uninstall the app. Uninstalling the app removes all stored data permanently.

## Children's Privacy

ILLLL does not collect data from anyone, including children. Since no data leaves the device, no age-specific data is collected or processed.

## Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted here with a new date.
