## The Short Version

ILLLL does not collect, store, or transmit any of your data. Period.

## What Data We Collect

None.

## What Data Is Stored On Your Device

ILLLL stores your habit tracking data (completion status, streaks, custom habits, preferences) locally on your device using encrypted storage. This data never leaves your device.

## Analytics & Tracking

None. ILLLL contains zero analytics, zero tracking pixels, zero crash reporting services, and zero third-party SDKs that collect data.

## Network Usage

ILLLL does not make any network requests. The app works entirely offline.

## Account

There is no account. There is no login. There is nothing to sign up for.

## Third-Party Services

ILLLL does not integrate with any third-party services that collect user data.

## Data Deletion

All your data is stored locally on your device. To delete it, use "Reset All Data" in Settings, or uninstall the app.

## Children's Privacy

ILLLL does not collect data from anyone, including children.

## Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted here with a new date.
